1. What advantages do cloud services like Netlify provide?

Netlify makes deployment easy, automatic and free. It provides a live website URL, handles updates from GitHub automatically and removes the need to manage servers.

2. How is this different from hosting a website on your local computer?

Hosting on a local computer means only you or people on your network can access it. With cloud hosting, your website is available online 24/7 for anyone in the world.
